About Rachel

Vancouver based graphic designer, and highly sought-after dinner guest.

Elsewhere:

She is available for hire for the following services:

Dreadful Art

Dreadful art for awful people.

Dreadful Art is a new art project I started at the beginning of 2015. I wanted to create a series of prints that satirize popular design trends, such as hand lettering, foiling, digital watercolour, "retro" effects, and type over the ubiquitous "nature photo". I will be updating the series periodically, and am selling the designs in various forms (multi-sized prints, canvas, shirts, totes, rugs, clocks, mugs, etc etc) online here.

  • Date: January 2015, onwards
  • Purchase: on Society6.com

Wedding Design

Various Clients

I was approached last year to create an invitation suite for my very good friends Tiffany and Michael. They're having a summer wedding, and wanted beautiful invitations to match their garden theme and colour scheme (lavender, buttercup, and sage). A few short chats and a visit to their beautiful community garden wedding venue later, we'd come up with the perfect design, which were printed on a beautiful, thick textured recycled cardstock. I couldn't have been happier with how they turned out!
Pictures of the invitation suite are forthcoming.

  • Date: October 2014
  • Clients: Tiffany & Michael

Alex Hass Design + Art

Website Design and Development

Alex and I worked together to create a new portfolio website to showcase her beautiful images and design work. Her main concern was displaying her work in a simple, clean way, that allowed the viewer to easily see her full body of work. As such, I came up with a simple static website that utilized a full-screen slideshow effect to seemlessly rotate between images and galleries, with an intuitive way to stop and examine each image fully without distracting navigational elements.

  • Date: March 2014
  • Client: Alex Hass
  • Website: alexhass.ca
